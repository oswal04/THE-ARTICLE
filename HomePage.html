The ARTICLE
The ARTICLE is a portal where you can look up news with respect to a lot of factors.

Article Search feature is, where you can enter a keyword or title of the news article that you wish to know
about which can date back to as old as 1851. It will fetch you the top 10 articles for the keyword.

Our NewsWire feature gives the latest news feeds with respect to different categories such as
Business News, Sports News, Arts News, etc. to name a few.

The What's Hot!! feature gives the news feeds that are trending with respect to sharing or emails or views
